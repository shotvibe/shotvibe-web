Frequently Asked Questions

What is Glance all about?

Glance is the first mobile service that lets you create albums on your mobile device, selecting the friends that you want to share the album with, and all the rest of your friends on the album can add their photos as well– so that the album you created is a "living album" – in a way that all the relevant pictures are there.

How do I register to Glance ?

Glance is the first albums mobile app ever that is fully based on your mobile phone number for registration. Once downloading the app – you'll sign up with your phone number. No email, password, Facebook, user name or anything else needed, as simple as that.

Do I need to connect with a Facebook account, twitter account, Email address, password or username to register on Glance ?

No, you will only sign in at the first usage using with your phone number.

Do I have to be always online/connected to view my albums on Glance ?

No – Glance is the first mobile service ever that will keep your photos for an offline view as well – wherever you are you'll have access to all of your album and all your photos. You can even create new albums and choose new friends to share the album with when you are offline and once your device gets a Wi-Fi or 3G/4G access it will upload the photos and videos to the cloud and share it with all your friends.

Can I add many photos to an album at once or do I have to select one picture at a time?

Yes – Glance lets you select multiple photos to upload, you don't need to select t one picture at a time. We are concerned that you need a fast service and don't have spare time to waste for selecting one picture at a time – like the rest of the services.

Can I upload videos to Glance?

Yes – Glance is your mobile media center for your pictures and videos – you can upload video to your album by choosing them from the phone gallery or you can shoot a video directly using the app – and the video will appear on your shared album.

Does Glance posts to Facebook or anywhere else on my behalf?

Glance will never post any of your pictures or videos to Facebook – we are not a social and a cool service for sharing photos with the rest of the world. We are a usability tool for creating albums and sharing them using your mobile device.

What is the contact list made of?

When you'll try to add new friends to your album – we'll present you the phone contact list – but we'll present only mobile phone number and nothing else – no home, office number, no fax, no email or nothing else – just your friend's mobile phone number. We are doing it using our sophisticated recognition algorithm. We want to make the invitation process as easy as possible for you – and we understand that presenting you all the unnecessary phone numbers is a waste of time.

How is it possible that the contact list is presenting only mobile phone numbers?

We are doing it using our sophisticated recognition algorithm. We want to make the process as easy as possible for you – and we understand that presenting you all the unnecessary phone numbers it's a waste of time.

What kind of invitation will the album member get once I have created an album?

When you select the friends you want to invite to the album created – we will recognize which one of your friends invited is a Glance registered member and which one is not registered user. All the registered members of the service will instantly get a push notification alerting them about the new album you have just created. All the unregistered members will get an SMS from Glance Company with a link – this link will lead your invited friends to a mobile mini site where they'll be able to see the album you just created. If they're interested in adding pictures to that album as well – then all they need to do is to press a button at the end of the mini-site leading them to the Apple store or Google play store.

How can I shoot pictures directly to an existing album?

Once you have created an album, you can either add pictures from your mobile phone galleries or you can add pictures directly by shooting pictures from the album. Once you are inside an album – you press the "+camera" button and the camera screen will appear, all the pictures taken now will be kept on the album and will be instantly shared with the rest of the friends in the album.

How do I create a new album on Glance ?

You simply press the "New album" button once you are in the main page of the app. You don't even need to name it – we will name it for you according to the date. If you wish to change it – you can do that. That's is – the album is ready – you can start uploading photos and videos to the album, and you can add friends to the album – it's that simple.

On which platforms Glance in working?

Glance is currently working on iOS, Android and web. You can use it also with your iPad, we are currently working on our unique tablets app – and we'll soon be running on windows phone and directly on PC and Mac.

How do I add friends to an album?

Once you're inside the album – you just press the friend's icon at the top-right part of the screen – then you'll see the full list of friends that are already part of that album. If you want to add more friends inside the album – then you simply press the "Add friends" button and then you'll see your phone contact list – not you can select each and every one of your friends from the contact list.

Where my photos are kept?

All your photos are backed in our server – available for you from each and every device you'll use. In addition, we are keeping all your photos locally on your mobile phone, compressed – so you won't need to be connected/online to view them – they're always available for you.

Will someone else except the album members see the album?

No. simply no. never. Your album photos will not appear on Facebook, or anywhere else – unless one of the album members decided to post them on Facebook – we will never pot something for you.

How can I invite a new friend to the app?

Inviting new friends to use the app is possible only after creating an album – once you have created an album – you select the friends you want to invite and send them an invitation. We are doing it since we don't want you just to invite more and more friends – we want the invited people to be part of an existing album.

Does Glance have a desktop application?

Currently no, but we are working on it. Due to be ready really soon. We'll update you about it.
