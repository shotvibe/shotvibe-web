About Glance

Glance is a fast and easy way to share photos and albums with close friends and private groups.

By tapping one time you can create a new album, upload photos and invite your "mini social" group.

Invite your friends to join your album using your phone contacts! Everyone invited to join the album will be available to add photos. Get instantly notifications when an image was uploaded, making this a truly living, sharable and private album!

Why Glance

  • All your pic and albums are stored forever in the cloud safely.
  • Get Unlimited Storage: Collect and store an unlimited amount of photos with Glance with no hassle.
  • It's private, fast and it’s the best way to share photos from phone to phone.
  • Available to Everyone. Glance is available on Android and iOS!

How People Use Glance?

Celebrating your birthday or wedding? Instantly collect every photo your guests took at your wedding or birthday.

Have kids in kindergarten or school? Create a group with the other parents and start share photos.

Camping trip or cycling tour? Collect photos from friends or strangers who went with you and send them as you wish.

From phone to phone photo transfer has never been easier!!
